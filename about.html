I design products at the intersection of business goals and human emotions. My happy place is the ones where a measurable metric moves—conversion, time on task, and more users—and semi-unsolicited thank-you messages start showing up.

Before design I trained as a psychology major, which still shows up in how I run research. I believe the best interviews feel like good conversations not interrogations.

When I am not in Figma, you will find me sketching, in cafes, running ultra marathons very slowly, or trying and failing to keep my plants alive.
